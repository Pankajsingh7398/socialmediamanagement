## Selected Design: Cyberpunk Minimalism

We are proceeding with **Cyberpunk Minimalism** — a high-tech, futuristic aesthetic that combines stark geometric precision with vibrant neon accents. This approach perfectly captures the innovative spirit of Rudraksha Pvt Ltd while maintaining professionalism and clarity.

### Design System Details

**Color Palette:**
- Background: Deep charcoal (#0F1419)
- Primary Text: Off-white (#F0F0F0)
- Accent 1 (Primary CTA): Neon cyan (#00D9FF)
- Accent 2 (Secondary): Electric magenta (#FF006E)
- Accent 3 (Success): Neon green (#39FF14)
- Neutral: Slate gray (#2D3748)

**Typography:**
- Headings: "Space Mono" 700 weight with 0.05em letter-spacing
- Body: "Inter" 400-500 weight
- Sizes: H1 (3.5rem), H2 (2.25rem), H3 (1.5rem), Body (1rem)

**Visual Elements:**
- Geometric SVG dividers with diagonal cuts
- Glassmorphism cards with 10% opacity backdrop blur
- Neon glowing borders on interactive elements
- Animated particles or scanlines in background
- Asymmetric grid layout with staggered sections

**Interactions:**
- Hover: Scale 1.05 + neon glow shadow
- Click: Instant feedback with scale transform
- Transitions: 300-400ms smooth easing
- Loading: Animated scanlines or pulsing elements

**Animations:**
- Entrance: Fade-in stagger from edges
- Scroll: Parallax on hero, fade-in stagger on cards
- Hover: Glow pulse, scale, color shift
- Exit: Fade-out with slight scale down
